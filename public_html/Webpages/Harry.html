Description

The Deathly Hallows is a children's tale, but Harry has never heard it. Hermione takes out the book that Dumbledore left her in his will and reads the entire tale to them. The story is about three brothers who meet Death on their road late one night. ... Death gives the third brother his Invisibility Cloak.
